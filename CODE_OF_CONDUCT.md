# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want InnoNetwork to be a respectful, technically rigorous, and inclusive project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical substance, not personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, but be precise about problems and tradeoffs.
- Keep discussions actionable and repository-focused.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or hostile language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated low-signal disruption after maintainer guidance

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation when behavior harms the project.

Security and conduct escalations should follow the channels in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) and [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
